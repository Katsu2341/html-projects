America

In Dota 2, American teams have historically been a significant part of the competitive scene. The region has seen some top-tier teams and players throughout the game's history, although many of the most prominent teams have fluctuated in terms of success over time. These teams are just a snapshot of the North American Dota 2 scene, which is still evolving. Historically, North American teams have often struggled to match the dominance of Southeast Asia, Europe, and China, but there have been periods where the region has had some of the strongest teams, particularly Evil Geniuses. In addition to these teams, many top Dota 2 players from the region also occasionally form smaller squads or shift between organizations depending on team dynamics and roster changes. If you’re looking for the latest teams or changes in the Dota 2 landscape, you can always check the latest DPC (Dota Pro Circuit) standings or follow major tournaments for updates on rosters and results.

Teams

Apex

Apex Genesis is a North American Dota 2 team established on March 17, 2024. The initial roster included lowskill, red2, Fayde, BliNcc, and Yamsun. By July 3, 2024, the lineup evolved to feature Sammyboy, W1sh-, Fayde, Bignum, and Yamsun, with just9th as coach.

Fart Studios

Fart Studios is a North American Dota 2 team formed in December 2022 to compete in the 2023 Dota Pro Circuit (DPC). The team was co-founded by Brian "BSJ" Canavan and Andrew "Jenkins" Jenkins, both of whom are well-known figures in the Dota 2 community as players, streamers, and analysts.

Shopify Rebellion

Shopify Rebellion is a Canadian esports organization that entered the Dota 2 scene in December 2022 by signing the former Evil Geniuses roster, along with offlaner Jonáš "SabeRLight-" Volek from TSM. This strategic move positioned them as a formidable force in the North American Dota 2 landscape.

Beastcoast

Beastcoast is a North American esports organization founded in July 2017. In May 2019, they entered the Dota 2 scene by signing the South American team Anvorgesa, marking their commitment to the region.

HERIOC

HEROIC, a Norwegian esports organization, entered the Dota 2 scene in January 2024 by signing a South American roster.The team quickly established itself as a dominant force in the South American region, consistently performing well in qualifiers.

Infamous

Infamous is a Peruvian esports organization established on December 15, 2015, by Christian "Xtian" Roque. The organization has been a significant presence in the South American Dota 2 scene, achieving several notable milestones.

Infinity

Infinity, formerly known as Infinity Esports, is a Costa Rican esports organization established in 2009. They entered the Dota 2 competitive scene in April 2020, marking their commitment to the game.

Lava Esports

Lava Esports is a Peruvian esports organization that entered the Dota 2 competitive scene in November 2021. The team has been an active participant in the South American Dota 2 circuit, competing in various regional tournaments and qualifiers.

Pacific Esports

Pacific Esports is a Bolivian Dota 2 team that has been active in the South American competitive scene. The team has participated in various regional tournaments and qualifiers, striving to establish itself among the prominent teams in the region.
